Privacy notice

Privacy

We respect your privacy.

Data we collect

  • Crash and stability diagnostics (anonymous) to help us fix bugs.
  • Anonymous usage counts (e.g. which references are opened most) to improve the library.

Data we do NOT collect

  • Patient information of any kind.
  • Personal identifiers (name, email, location, contacts).
  • Anything you type into the search box or notes.

Where data lives

  • Your bookmarks and downloaded documents stay on your device.
  • Diagnostic data is processed by our hosting provider and is not sold or shared with third parties for advertising.

Your choices

  • You can clear local data at any time by uninstalling the app.
  • You can request deletion of any diagnostic data linked to your install by emailing zambiaanaesthesiahub@gmail.com.

Children

This app is intended for clinical professionals and is not directed at children.

Changes

We will notify you in-app when this notice is updated.
