Offline-first reference for anaesthesia practitioners in Zambia.

About the app

Zambia Anaesthesia Hub is a non-commercial reference tool curated for anaesthesia practitioners working in Zambia. It bundles emergency algorithms, departmental checklists, and clinical guidelines so they remain available even without an internet connection.

The app is built and maintained by clinicians for clinicians. Please report any inaccuracies, suggested additions, or technical issues to the support contact below.
