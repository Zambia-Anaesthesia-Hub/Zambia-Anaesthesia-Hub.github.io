About

The Zambia Anaesthesia Hub is a non-commercial reference tool curated for anaesthesia practitioners working in Zambia.

It is built and maintained by clinicians for clinicians, and aims to keep critical, locally-relevant information available offline at the bedside.

For corrections, content suggestions, or technical support, contact zambiaanaesthesiahub@gmail.com.
